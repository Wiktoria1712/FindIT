How it works

Simple for shoppers, valuable for small local stores

01

Enter a product

The shopper types a name, size, or brand, and the app matches it to the product catalog.

02

The platform checks inventory from independent shops through API, POS tools, or simple stock sync exports.

03

PWA schema

How this app database works

products

Stores the main products, their brand, variant, size, barcode, and category. This is the central search layer.

stores

Contains stores, location, opening hours, store type, coordinates, and integration settings for each retail partner.

inventory_snapshots

Holds current stock levels for a product in a specific store together with price, sync timestamp, and availability status.

reservations and search_events

Reservations help customers secure a product, while search logs reveal demand patterns and support retailer analytics.
